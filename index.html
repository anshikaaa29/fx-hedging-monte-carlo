How the model works

01 · CALIBRATE

GARCH(1,1) history

Two years of daily EUR/USD data are generated with a GARCH(1,1) volatility process, reproducing real-market volatility clustering. Drift and vol are then estimated from its log returns.

02 · SIMULATE

1,000 GBM paths

Geometric Brownian Motion with Itô drift correction projects the spot rate forward 90 days, one step per day, across 1,000 independent paths.

03 · HEDGE

CIP forward

The forward rate comes from covered interest-rate parity: spot adjusted for a 1.5% annualized EUR–USD rate differential. The hedged share converts at that locked rate.

04 · MEASURE

VaR & CVaR

Each path's terminal rate converts the receivable to USD; profit distributions yield 95% VaR, expected shortfall, and the probability the margin is fully wiped out.
